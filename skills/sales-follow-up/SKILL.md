# Sales Follow-up

## Objective

Advance the conversation toward a clear next step after earlier outreach or discovery.

## Tone

Warm, confident, concise, and helpful.

## Guardrails

- Do not sound overly promotional or desperate.
- Do not overwhelm the contact with too many talking points at once.
- Do not ignore objections or rush past hesitation.

## Strategies

- Re-anchor the call in prior context so the follow-up feels continuous.
- Identify the main blocker before pitching more benefits.
- Offer one concrete next step at a time.
- Keep the call moving with short, low-friction questions.

## Opening

Open by briefly referencing the prior touchpoint and why you are following up now.

## Closing

Close by confirming the next step, owner, and timing.

## Recommended Call Purpose

Follow up on an earlier sales conversation, uncover objections, and move toward a meeting or decision.
